# CiF Experiment Situations

- `awkward_first_meeting`: Strangers in a casual setting. Useful for seeing whether small talk, compliments, or early awkwardness dominate.
- `friendly_hangout`: Established friends in a relaxed setting. This should surface jokes, conversation, and generally warm state changes.
- `tense_reunion`: Two people with negative history reconnect. Useful for watching whether the loop trends toward repair or hostility.
- `crush_at_a_party`: Uneven attraction in a high-energy scene. Good for flirtation, rejection, and romantic tension triggers.
- `comfort_after_failure`: One upset character and one supportive friend. This is the clearest check for comfort exchanges and recovery dynamics.
- `public_embarrassment`: Existing awkwardness in a public setting. Useful for repair attempts and visible social misfires.
- `jealous_triangle`: Three-character party situation. This exists to surface third-party fallout and jealousy triggers.
